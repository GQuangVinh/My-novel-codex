# Kael

**Species & Appearance:**
Grey wolf, mid-twenties. Lean, not muscular — the kind of thin that comes from forgetting to eat. A scar bisects his left eyebrow, pulling it into a permanent question. Wears the same jacket regardless of weather; it smells like woodsmoke and someone else's perfume he won't wash out.

**Voice Sample:**
> "Yeah, no, I heard you. I just — can we not do this right now?"
> "You want me to say I'm sorry? Fine. I'm sorry. Does that fix anything? Does that un-break a single—" *[stops. Breathes. Quieter.]* "...I'm sorry."

**The Wound:**
Abandoned by his mother at seven. Left at a neighbor's door with a note he never read because he couldn't read yet.

**The Mask:**
Aggressively self-sufficient. Refuses help before it's offered. Makes everything a joke before it can become serious. "I'm fine" is his most fluent lie.

**The Tell:**
When someone does something genuinely kind — unprompted, no strings — he goes completely still. Just for a second. Like his body forgot what to do with it.

**Contradiction:**
Desperately wants to be known ↔ Pushes away everyone who gets close enough to see him.

**Comfort Object/Habit:**
Counts things when anxious — ceiling tiles, steps, breaths. Not OCD, just... the numbers are reliable when nothing else is.

**He would NEVER:**
Ask someone to stay.

**He can't help but:**
Check every exit in a room before sitting down. Not danger — just the need to know he can leave.
